Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know but were afraid to ask.

How do I get to the ceremony?

Please make your own way to Conversano for the ceremony and we'll take care of everything from there. If you're renting a car, do be careful — there are Italian drivers out there, and we want you at the wedding in one piece!

What will the weather be like?

Hopefully glorious Pugliese sunshine — and it almost certainly will be. But even if the weather has other ideas, the party absolutely goes ahead regardless. Pack light layers just in case, but don't let it worry you.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely — the venue will be providing entertainment for the little ones throughout the day. We haven't been given the full details of what's planned, so we're choosing to imagine it's Battle Royale style. Whatever it is, they'll be well looked after.

Can I bring a plus one?

If you're planning to bring a plus one, please do let us know when you RSVP — just include their name and details in your response so we can make sure we have them on the list.

I'm struggling to find accommodation — help!

We've put together some accommodation suggestions on the Stay page — have a look there first. If you're still stuck, please don't hesitate to get in touch directly and we'll do our best to point you in the right direction.

When will the party end?

We suspect it'll go well into the small hours of the morning — this is a wedding in Italy after all. A word to the wise: if you're not a fan of loud music late at night, perhaps don't book a room at the reception venue itself.

Is there a free bar?

Not once we're finished with it.

Was this website made by an AI?

Yes — Claude helped enormously. We've probably put some poor web designer out of a job, and we're not sure we could have done it without it given the time available. But here we are, and we think it looks rather good.
