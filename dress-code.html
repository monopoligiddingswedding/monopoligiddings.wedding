Dress Code

We want you to look and feel fabulous. Here's what we have in mind.

Suited & Booted

We're celebrating in style in the Pugliese sunshine — smart formal dress is the order of the day. Think suits, ties, elegant dresses and heels. This is a big occasion and we'd love everyone to dress the part.

👔

For the Gentlemen

Suits and ties please. A classic look — smart, sharp and celebration-ready. Pocket squares encouraged.

👗

For the Ladies

Elegant dresses or formal suits. Heels, wedges or smart flats — bear in mind we'll be on a Masseria, so stilettos on cobblestones are at your own risk!

🌡️

Bear in Mind

Puglia in summer is gloriously hot. Light fabrics are your friends — linen suits, breathable materials and layers you can remove as the evening warms up.

🎩

Big Silly Hats: Strongly Encouraged

This is not a drill. If you own a wonderfully oversized, outrageous or frankly inexplicable hat, this is your moment. Extra points for creativity. The bigger, the better.
