Getting There & Staying Over

We're getting married in the beautiful region of Puglia in southern Italy. Here's everything you need to plan your trip.

Flying in

Getting to Puglia

There are two airports serving the area. Both are well connected from major UK and European cities, and either will get you to Conversano with ease. We recommend booking flights early as summer availability in Puglia fills up quickly.

BRI

Bari Karol Wojtyła Airport

Approximately 35–40 minutes from Conversano by car. The larger of the two airports with more direct flight options from the UK and across Europe.

BDS

Brindisi Airport

Approximately 60–70 minutes from Conversano by car. A good alternative, particularly if you find better flight connections or pricing.

On the day

Getting Around

Getting yourself to Conversano is down to you — we recommend hiring a car, which also lets you explore the stunning Pugliese countryside at your own pace. Once the celebrations begin, we've got you covered.

Your own arrangements

Airport → Conversano

Please make your own way to Conversano. We recommend hiring a car — it gives you the freedom to explore Puglia before and after the wedding. Taxis and transfers are also available from both airports.
